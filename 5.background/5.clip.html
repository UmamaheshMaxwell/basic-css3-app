Background Clip Property

The background-clip property defines how far the background should extend with in element

Background-clip: border-box ( this is default)

The background extends behind the border

Background-clip : padding-box

The background extneds to the inside edge of the border

Background-clip: content-box

The background extends to the edge of the cotent box
